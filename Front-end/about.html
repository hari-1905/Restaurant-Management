About Us

Cooking Since 1943

Simmakkal Konar Kadai,established in 1943 by the late Shri Sundara Konar,beag as the modest eatery in the simmakal area of Madurai.The local community affectionately named it as "Konar Kadai" in his honor,a name that has endured over the decades.

The restaurant is reowned for its authentic South Indian non-veg cuisine,with signature dishes like kari dosa - a layered dosa with mutton gravy and egg-earning widespread acclaim. Over time,Simmakkal konar kadai has expanded its presence,opening additonal branches in Madurai and extended to cities like Chennai.
