BREAKFAST

Enjoy a delicious breakfast that are freshly prepared to energize your morning. From soft idlis and crispy dosas to flavorful pongal and vada served with an array of chutneys and sambar.

LUNCH

Enjoy a flavorful lunch with Konar Kadai’s authentic South Indian dishes. From aromatic biryanis and spicy curries to soft parottas, every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional spices.

DINNER

End your day with a delicious dinner at Konar Kadai. Indulge in a variety of South Indian specialties like Kari Dosai, biryanis, parottas, chickhen & mutton gravy, flavorful curries all prepared fresh with authentic spices.
